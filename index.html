Frequently Asked Questions

How much does dog daycare cost in Waterkloof Ridge? +

Dog daycare is R120 per day. Includes supervised group play, outdoor and indoor space, and no hidden fees.

How much does dog boarding cost in Pretoria? +

Boarding is R200 per 24 hours, day and night included. Discounts for long stays and multiple dogs available. WhatsApp us for a quote.

What days and hours are you open? +

We are open 7 days a week, including weekends and public holidays, from 07:00 to 18:00.

Is my dog safe at daycare? +

Yes. All dogs are supervised in a fully fenced property. We do a meet and greet before any dog joins to keep the group safe and compatible.

How do I book a spot? +

WhatsApp us or call 071 117 2998 to check availability. We'll set up a meet and greet before your dog's first day.

Where are you located? +

We are located in Waterkloof Ridge, Pretoria, Gauteng (0181). Contact us for the exact address once you've booked your meet and greet.
