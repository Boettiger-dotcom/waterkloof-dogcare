FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your dog in.

How much does dog daycare cost in Waterkloof Ridge? +

Dog daycare is R110 per day. This includes a full day of supervised group play, access to a spacious outdoor play area and indoor rest space, and plenty of love and attention. There are no hidden fees.

How much does dog boarding cost in Pretoria? +

Boarding is R200 per 24 hours — full day and night included. Long stay discounts and rates for multiple dogs are available — WhatsApp us for a custom quote.

What days and hours are you open? +

We are open 7 days a week, including weekends and public holidays, from 07:00 to 18:00.

Is my dog safe at daycare? +

Yes. All dogs are supervised at all times in a fully fenced property with a large outdoor play area and a sheltered indoor rest space. We do a meet and greet before any dog joins to ensure a safe, compatible group environment.

How do I book a spot? +

Simply WhatsApp us or call 071 117 2998 to check availability. We'll set up a meet and greet before your dog's first day.

Where are you located? +

We are located in Waterkloof Ridge, Pretoria, Gauteng (0181). Contact us for the exact address once you've booked your meet and greet.
